Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about Bitcoin security and insurance

How does BlocentraAI detect wallet risks?
Our AI analyzes multiple risk factors including transaction patterns, wallet age, connected services, smart contract interactions, historical breaches, and phishing exposure. We combine on-chain analytics with machine learning to identify potential threats before they materialize.
What does Bitcoin insurance cover?
Our insurance covers wallet breaches (hacking), smart contract failures, bridge vulnerabilities, phishing attacks, and private key compromise. Coverage varies by plan, with our Advanced Guard plan providing the most comprehensive protection including DeFi-related risks.
How are insurance premiums calculated?
Premiums are based on your wallet's risk score, coverage amount, and selected plan. Lower risk scores result in lower premiums. Our dynamic pricing model adjusts as your risk profile changes, rewarding good security practices.
Can I insure Bitcoin stored on exchanges?
Currently, we only insure self-custodied Bitcoin wallets. Exchange-held Bitcoin is covered by the exchange's insurance policies. We focus on providing security for wallets where you control the private keys.
How quickly are claims processed?
Valid claims are typically processed within 2 business days. We use blockchain forensics to verify claims, and our AI systems help accelerate the verification process. Institutional clients have expedited processing options.
